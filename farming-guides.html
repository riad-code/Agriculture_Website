Mango Farming: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose a sunny location with well-drained soil.
Step 2: Plant mango saplings with proper spacing of 10-15 feet.
Step 3: Provide adequate watering and mulch for moisture retention.
Tips: Protect trees from extreme weather conditions.
Best Practice: Prune regularly to ensure proper airflow and sunlight penetration.
